A それで/ですからB

Because of A, B happen, A is cause and reason of B

ですから→ ですからhas clear causal relationship between A and B
それで→それでis not so clear and doesn't have strong causal relationship than ですから. Sometimes it just means to move sentences.
(それで? is small response when people are talking, one person wants to get more progress of conversation. = So?, And?)

めったに/なかなか

めったに→めったに〜ない。frequency word (How often~?) seldom、hardly
なかなか→
① more 〜 than my expectation / more 〜 than I thought. (I can use both positive and negative situation.)
②なかなか〜ない。Things are worse than I thought. Things are not so more progress than my expectation.

いつも/いつでも/いつか

いつも and いつでも are almost same meaning. But only いつでも has meaning “at any time”
いつか → unclear point in the future or past.

かばん/ポケット

かばん→ bag
ポケット→ pocket
Are these two words really same word (Tasche) in your world?

よていひょう/よてい/スケジュール

よてい＝スケジュール plan
よていひょう is paper or table of よてい/スケジュール.

キッチン/だいどころ

だいどころand キッチン have almost the same meaning. Now just it's difference between Western and Japanese styles of words. Sensorily, だいどころ is a room for cooking, キッチン is place for cooking, but it doesn't have a big difference.

ちきゅう/つち

ちきゅう→earth
つち→soil

それに/ほかに

それに→ also, to add
ほかに→ moreover
Both are used to add information. But depend on the situation.
The most popular situation of ほかに, 「ほかに しつもん は ありますか？」"Do you have any other questions?"

さします/みせます

さします→ point
＊ゆび を さします。(point fingers)
＊とけい の はり が 10じ を さします。(The hands of the clock point to ten o'clock = It's ten o'clock.)
It's almost only used in situations of fingers and hands of clock.
みせます→ show
＊わたし は あなた に しゃしん を みせます。(will show you pictures.)

きゅうけいします/やすみます

きゅうけいする→ きゅうけいする means Pause from only work or school situations. (between work time and work time, between class and class.) Also we can use Pause for hiking or sport.
やすみます → やすみます also includes きゅうけいします。やすみますhas meaning of Uelaub, absent.

みつけます/ひろいます

みつけます→find, find out, discover
ひろいます→ ひろいます is action of picking up something, picking up something from ground. (When you drop food, When you pick up trash on the street)

ふくざつ/むずかしい

ふくざつ→complicated, situations or relationships are complicated and difficult to understand or explain easily
むずかしい→difficult, むずかしい means difficult to understand or learn. Because of ふくざつ, hard to understand.

つれてきます/もってきます/つれていきます/もっていきます

＊つれて→ only use for person (sometimes animals or pets)
＊もって→ for objects

つれてきます→ ママ は こども を つれてきます。(Mama brings her child, Mama come with her child.)
もってきます→ かのじょ は たべもの を もってきます。(She brings some food.)
つれていきます→ わたし は いぬ を こうえん へ つれていきます。(I take my dog to the park, I go to the park with my dog.)
もっていきます → わたしはパーティーにZucchinistrudelをもっていきます。(I bring the Zucchinistrudel to the party. I join the party with Zucchinistrudel)
